Referencing

Introduction

Referencing means acknowledging your source. When you write academic assignments, you must demonstrate your wide reading by ensuring that various ideas or concepts are cited appropriately.

If you would like more information, please contact staff from the Academic Language and Learning Success Program (ALLSP), who run free workshops available for internal and external students on Harvard and APA styles.
