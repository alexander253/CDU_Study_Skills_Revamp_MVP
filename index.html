Referencing

Introduction

Referencing means acknowledging your source. When you write academic assignments, you must demonstrate your wide reading by citing ideas or concepts from this wide reading.

If you would like more information, please contact staff from The Academic Language and Learning Success Program (ALLSP), run free workshops for internal and external students on Harvard and APA styles.
